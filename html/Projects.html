Photography- Reimagined Spaces

The prompt of this project was to photograph two spaces, one at Fordham University and one somewhere in NYC, and reimagine it be more interesting or useful.
I took 2 pictures, one wide and one close up, and used Adobe Photoshop to reimagine it.
My two subjects were Argo Tea and Utopia Park, two places I have frequently visited and thought they could be improved upon. For the imagined Argo Tea photos, I thought it would be a great space to inspire a little donation with a coin collector and added a little bit of fun in the space with a floor projector. For the imagined Utopia Park photos, I added a formal running trail to help the runners have a space dedicated for them.

Videography- Documentary

In this project, I worked with my partner Marlena to conceptualize and film before solo editing a cohesive documentary.

The premise of the film is based on the differences between me and Marlena.
I am a commuter student attending at the Lincoln Center campus while Marlena attends at the Rose Hill campus.
Despite the differences between location and schedule, we both are able to come together as a part of Fordham University.
